Based on the detected interaction between Lisinopril and Ibuprofen, our clinical team has prepared the following resolution plan.

What you should do next

Priority: Urgent
1

Pause this for now

Discontinue the use of Ibuprofen immediately. The combination can significantly reduce the effectiveness of your blood pressure medication and strain kidney function.

2

Try this instead

For pain relief, use Acetaminophen (Tylenol) as it does not interact with ACE inhibitors. Limit dosage to no more than 3,000mg per 24 hours.

3

Keep an eye on your BP

Record your blood pressure twice daily for the next 72 hours. If systolic pressure exceeds 160mmHg, contact your physician immediately.
