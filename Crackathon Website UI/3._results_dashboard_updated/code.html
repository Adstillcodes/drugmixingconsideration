Warfarin ↔ Ciprofloxacin

priority_high Increased Risk of Bleeding (Major Severity)

expand_more

Ciprofloxacin may inhibit the metabolism of Warfarin, leading to significantly elevated INR levels and increased hemorrhage risk. This interaction is well-documented and typically requires dosage adjustment or intensive monitoring.

Please follow these steps for your well-being

  • task_alt Monitor INR daily for first 72h
  • task_alt Reduce Warfarin dose by 25-33%
